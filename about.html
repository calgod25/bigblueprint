Who is Cal

Calvin Godfrey is a USA-Palestinian professional basketball player known for his versatility, athleticism, and resilience. Having played both domestically and internationally, Cal brings real-world experience and insight to the court.

He played Division I basketball at Iowa State and Southern University. His professional career has taken him across the globe, where he's earned a reputation as a dependable and impactful big man.

Drawing from his own journey—starting as a raw, tall, under-coached young athlete—Cal founded The BIG Blueprint to bridge the gap in training for Power Forwards and Centers. This program is his way of giving back, shaping the next generation of elite bigs who are smart, skilled, and positionally confident.
