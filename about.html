About Me

Calvin Godfrey is an American-Palestinian professional basketball player who has built a career competing internationally since his collegiate days at Southern University and Iowa State. Known for his versatile play at the forward and center positions, he has gained recognition for his athleticism, basketball IQ, and commitment to mastering the fundamentals of the game.

Over the years, Calvin has played across multiple countries including Finland, Saudi Arabia, Morocco, Iraq, Tunisia, and Malaysia, adapting his game and growing his impact on and off the court. His experiences in various leagues and coaching systems inspired him to build "The BIG Blueprint" — a program rooted in clarity, consistency, and coaching tailored to bigs.

With years of professional experience and a drive to give back, Calvin is committed to guiding the next generation of tweeners, power forwards, and centers to carve out their lane and take control of their basketball future.
