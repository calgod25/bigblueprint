Welcome to The BIG Blueprint

Created for the often-overlooked bigs — power forwards, centers, and tweeners who need dedicated, fundamental training to elevate their game and get seen.

My Story

I was a scrawny, taller-than-average kid who never quite fit into the traditional training mold. I tagged along in workouts meant for guards or wings, but knew I’d one day be a forward or center. So I trained myself — parks, gyms, whatever it took to polish the skills I needed. The BIG Blueprint is the program I wish I had growing up. Built for you.

Our Mission

To give young bigs the training, tools, and clarity they need to succeed in today’s evolving game. While the NBA is more dynamic than ever, one thing stays the same — being elite in your role matters. Let’s define it together.

Why I’m Here for You

  • Position-specific drills and IQ
  • Confidence through repetition
  • Adaptability for modern basketball
  • Insight from a pro who’s lived it

Is This for You?

If you're a tall kid who’s always played “everywhere,” this is your lane. If you’ve been told to just run the floor, set screens, or stand in the paint — and you want more — you’re in the right place. This is for the kids and families who want to define their role, refine their tools, and elevate their chances of being seen.
